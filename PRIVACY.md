# ZabkaFinder — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 2026_

## What data the app uses

ZabkaFinder uses your watch's **GPS location** for one purpose only:
finding Zabka stores near you.

## What leaves your device

When the app searches for stores, it sends your **approximate
location** (a ~1 km bounding box around your GPS position) to the
**Nominatim** search service operated by the OpenStreetMap Foundation
(nominatim.openstreetmap.org). This request is necessary to return
nearby store locations. It is covered by the OSMF privacy policy:
https://osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy

No other data is transmitted anywhere.

## What we collect and store

**Nothing.** ZabkaFinder has no accounts, no analytics, no ads, no
tracking, and no servers of its own. Your location is never stored,
logged, or shared by the app's developer. All distance and direction
calculations happen on the watch.
